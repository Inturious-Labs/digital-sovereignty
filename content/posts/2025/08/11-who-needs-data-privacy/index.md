When friends from big tech or traditional business hear that I work in Web3, a quick debate is surely in order, which often starts from soul-searching challenge “why YOU PEOPLE in Web3 care so much about data privacy” and moves to an astounding claim, “the history of the Internet has proven that people don’t care about data privacy but just comfort and convenience”. Welcome to my life! I get this a lot.
